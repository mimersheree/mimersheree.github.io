Online Grocery Shopping Platform

Project Duration

3 months

Problem Statement

The problem with online grocery shopping platforms was the usability and engagement with ordering groceries online. This was definitely more prominent after COVID where users transitioned to online groceries as it was more accessible. The purpose of redesigning an online grocery shopping platform was to increase user engagement and create a seamless experience for users.

The Solution

  • Added different categories for users to navigate from the homepage to their designated interests
  • Included features for generated recipes based on previous shopping experience and purchases
